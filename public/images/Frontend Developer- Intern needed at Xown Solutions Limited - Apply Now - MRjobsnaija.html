    Job title : Frontend Developer- Intern

    Job Location : Lagos

    Deadline : June 26, 2023

    Quick Recommended Links

    About the job

    • As a Frontend Developer, you will play a key role in developing and maintaining both new and existing web applications.
    • You will collaborate closely with our design and backend teams to create visually stunning and highly functional user interfaces.
    • The ideal candidate will be passionate about frontend development, possess strong problem-solving skills, and have a deep understanding of modern web technologies

    Responsibilities
    • Develop and implement responsive and user-friendly web applications using JavaScript, HTML, NextJS, CSS, and Sass.
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate UI/UX design wireframes and concepts into clean and efficient code.
    • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
    • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.
    • Stay up to date with emerging frontend technologies and industry best practices.
    • Conduct code reviews to maintain code quality, performance, and adherence to coding standards
    • Troubleshoot and debug issues, providing timely resolutions.
    • Collaborate with backend developers to integrate frontend and backend functionalities.
    • Actively participate in agile development processes, including sprint planning, daily stand-ups, and retrospectives.

    Requirements

    • BSc or HND in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
    • Strong proficiency in React and it’s core principles
    • Proficient understanding of web markup, including HTML5 and CSS3.
    • Experience with CSS preprocessors, such as Sass
    • Solid understanding of responsive design principles and mobile-first development.
    • Familiarity with frontend build tools, such as Webpack or Gulp
    • Knowledge of version control systems, preferably Git.
    • Familiarity with RESTful APIs and asynchronous request handling
    • Ability to write clean, efficient, and maintainable code
    • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
    • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
    • Ability to adapt to changing priorities and work well under pressure
    • Applicant should not be above 25years of age

    Preferred Skills

    • Familiarity with server-side rendering and Next.js.
    • Knowledge of UI/UX design principles and an eye for aesthetics.

    How to Apply for this Offer

    Please submit your resume, portfolio, and any relevant code samples demonstrating your skills to careers@xownsolutions.com
